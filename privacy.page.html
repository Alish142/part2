Privacy and Security
Data Collection and Use

In compliance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), we collect only the personal information necessary to provide you with our services. This includes details such as your name, email address, and workout preferences. This information is used exclusively for creating and managing your personalized workout plans and improving the app's functionality.

Data Protection

We adhere to industry-standard security practices to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, interference, loss, alteration, or disclosure. Your data is securely stored on servers located in compliance with Australian data protection regulations and encrypted during transmission to ensure its safety.

Third-Party Sharing

Your personal information will not be shared with third parties except in circumstances required by Australian law or with your explicit consent. We ensure that any third-party service providers comply with equivalent data protection standards.

Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, you can contact us directly through the app or by sending an email to our support team. We aim to respond to your requests promptly and transparently.

If you have concerns about how we manage your personal information, you also have the right to contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

Complaints and Feedback

If you believe we have breached the Australian Privacy Act or the APPs, please contact us to resolve your concerns. We take privacy matters seriously and aim to address complaints in a timely and satisfactory manner.
